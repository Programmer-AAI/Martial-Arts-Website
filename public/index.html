Our Martial Arts Disciplines

Master multiple combat systems under one roof

Sanda (Chinese Kickboxing)

Chinese full-contact combat sport combining punching, kicking, and throwing techniques.

  • Practical striking
  • Punch and Kick Blocking
  • Competitive sparring
  • Every friday full-contact competitive fight

Trained in: All regular classes

Taolu (Forms)

Pre-arranged sequences of techniques demonstrating perfect form and combat applications.

  • Modern taulo forms
  • Weapon training
  • Competition training
  • Dificulty Kicks

Focus days: All regular classes

Reality Self-Defense

Practical techniques for real-world situations including weapons defense and multiple attackers.

  • Scenario training
  • Self-Defence technique
  • Practical Knowledge

Monthly workshop: Once in a month
